About US

Who are we? Kayak-Ibiza is formed by an international and multidisciplinary team, passionate about outdoor activities. With a lot of experience leading groups in Ibiza. We know the island like the back of our hands!! We are fascinated by the turquoise hues of the waters that surround us. The caves, the beaches only accessible via the sea, and the hidden coves keep surprising us. With us, you will be in the safest and best of hands! We adapt the excursions to your level and we have the appropriate material for it.
